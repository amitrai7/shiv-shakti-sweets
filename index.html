About Shiv Shakti Sweets

Purity, Quality, and Tradition since 1984.

For over four decades, Shiv Shakti Sweets has been sweetening your celebrations and daily moments. Our journey started in a small kitchen with a promise—to never compromise on the quality and purity of what we offer.

Every batch of our sweets is crafted by skilled Halwais (traditional confectioners) using traditional techniques, 100% pure milk fat (Desi Ghee), and fresh premium dry fruits. No artificial preservatives or flavor enhancers find a place in our kitchens.

100% Desi Ghee

Rich taste and premium texture in every bite.

No Preservatives

Freshly prepared daily with completely natural ingredients.

Generational Recipes

Time-tested methods passed down through families.

Purity Certified

Strict quality controls ensuring safe and delicious sweets.
